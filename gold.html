GOLD

Most mined gold is stored as bullion. It is also, however, used extensively in jewellery, either in its pure form or as an alloy. .It is attractive in colour and brightness, durable to the point of virtual indestructibility, highly malleable, and usually found in nature in a comparatively pure form. The history of gold is unequaled by that of any other metal because of its perceived value from earliest times.
